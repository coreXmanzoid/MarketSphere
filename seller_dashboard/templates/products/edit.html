Product Tips

  • Lead your title with the brand and the most searched keyword.
  • Keep short descriptions under 20 words — they show on crowded cards.
  • Mention material, size, and compatibility in the full description.

Image Recommendations

  • Use a plain or white background for the primary image.
  • Include at least one lifestyle or in-use photo.
  • Square images (1:1) display most consistently across the site.

SEO Tips

  • Keep meta titles under 60 characters so they aren't truncated.
  • Front-load meta descriptions with the key benefit.
  • Avoid keyword stuffing — write for shoppers, not algorithms.
